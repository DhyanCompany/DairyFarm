Our Story of Pure Dairy

For over 15 years, Santok Dairy Farm has been committed to providing pure, organic dairy products to families across Gujarat. Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to fresh, unadulterated dairy products.

Our Journey Our Values

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to Gujarat's trusted dairy farm

2009: The Beginning

Started with just 10 cows and a dream to provide pure milk to our local community in Mehsana. Our focus was always on quality over quantity.

2012: First Expansion

Expanded our farm to 50 cows and started supplying milk to neighboring towns. Introduced our first ghee production using traditional methods.

2015: Organic Certification

Received organic certification for all our products. Implemented sustainable farming practices and started delivering to Ahmedabad.

2018: Modernization

Upgraded our facilities with modern milking equipment while preserving traditional processing methods. Started home delivery service.

2021: Digital Presence

Launched our website and online ordering system. Expanded delivery to Surat and Deesa, serving over 5000 happy families.

2024: Today & Beyond

Operating with 500+ happy cows, delivering fresh dairy to 4 cities, and constantly innovating while staying true to our traditional values.
