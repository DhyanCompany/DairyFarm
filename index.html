The Magical Journey

From milk to ghee - A transformation of purity

1

Fresh Milk Collection

Our journey begins at dawn with the collection of fresh, organic milk from our grass-fed cows. The milk is tested for purity and quality before it begins its transformation.

  • Collected within 2 hours of milking
  • Temperature-controlled storage
  • Regular quality checks
2

Natural Cream Separation

The milk is left to rest naturally, allowing the rich cream to separate and rise to the top. This traditional method preserves the natural nutrients and authentic flavor.

  • No mechanical separation
  • 12-hour natural process
  • Preserves natural enzymes
3

Slow Cooking Magic

The cream is slowly cooked over traditional fire in copper vessels. This slow cooking process transforms cream into golden ghee while developing its rich aroma.

  • 4-6 hours slow cooking
  • Traditional copper vessels
  • Constant temperature monitoring
